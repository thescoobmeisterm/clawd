# Apollo’s Learning Corner — Pricing + Offer Ladder (Draft)

**Goal:** Convert parents from free → low ticket → core offer → premium.

---

## Offer Ladder (Recommended)

| Tier | Offer | Target Price | What’s Included | Why It Works |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| **Free** | “Space Adventure” Lead Magnet Printable | $0 | 1‑page printable (count/trace/draw) | Captures email + builds trust fast |
| **Low Ticket** | Printable Pack (Theme Pack) | $7–$12 | 10–20 printables (activities, tracing, puzzles) | Easy impulse buy, high margin |
| **Core** | Monthly Learning Club | $15–$25/mo | 4 new printables + 1 mini video + parent guide | Recurring revenue + retention |
| **Mid** | Themed Learning Bundle | $29–$49 | Full themed pack (printables + 3 short videos) | Clear value + gifting potential |
| **Premium** | “Apollo Adventures” Course + Kit | $79–$149 | 4‑week lesson plan + printable kit | Higher AOV, deeper engagement |

---

## Sample Offer Names
- **Free:** “Apollo’s Space Adventure Starter Page”
- **Low:** “Mission Pack: Space Week”
- **Core:** “Apollo Learning Club”
- **Mid:** “Galaxy Explorer Bundle”
- **Premium:** “Apollo Adventures: 4‑Week Space Quest”

---

## Key Notes
- Anchor **Core** as best value (monthly)
- Keep **Low** offer below $12 to reduce friction
- Use **Premium** as upsell for parents who want structure
- Bundle videos with printables for clear differentiation
