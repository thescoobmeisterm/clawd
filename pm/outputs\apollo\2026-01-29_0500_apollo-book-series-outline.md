# Children’s Book Series (Apollo) — Series Outline v1

## Series Premise
Apollo, a curious young dog, learns big life lessons through small neighborhood adventures.

## Core Goals
| Goal | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Build character + habits | Repeatable lessons for kids |
| Strong visuals | Easy to illustrate + animate |
| Short, teachable arcs | 10–12 page books |

## Main Characters
| Character | Role | Traits |
|---|---|---|
| Apollo | Protagonist | Curious, brave, kind |
| Mom/Dad | Guide | Encouraging, steady |
| Neighbor Kids | Friends | Playful, diverse |
| Mentor (Mr. Oak) | Wisdom | Gentle, quirky |

## Book Arc (5‑Book Starter Set)
| # | Title | Lesson | Hook |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Apollo’s Big Day | Courage | First day jitters |
| 2 | Apollo Shares the Sandbox | Sharing | Learning to take turns |
| 3 | Apollo Tries Again | Persistence | Failing + retrying |
| 4 | Apollo Helps a Friend | Empathy | Helping in small ways |
| 5 | Apollo’s Quiet Time | Calm | Self‑regulation |

## Episode Template (Reusable)
1. **Problem:** Apollo faces a relatable kid‑moment.
2. **Attempt:** He tries a quick fix.
3. **Mentor Moment:** A gentle nudge.
4. **Practice:** Apollo tries again.
5. **Win:** Small success + gratitude.

## Visual Style Notes
- Warm, soft palettes; neighborhood settings.
- Simple expressions; big emotional cues.
- Reuse props (park bench, tree, sandbox).
