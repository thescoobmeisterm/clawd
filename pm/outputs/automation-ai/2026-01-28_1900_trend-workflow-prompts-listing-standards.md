# Trend Workflow + Prompt Pack + Listing Standards (T-Shirts)

## 1) Weekly Trend Research Workflow (T-0022)

### Objective
Identify 5–10 trend-worthy concepts weekly, ranked by market demand + competition, and output 3 production‑ready design briefs.

### Cadence (Weekly)
| Day | Step | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | Collect signals (trend scan) | Raw trend list (20–30 ideas) |
| Tue | Validate demand (search + marketplace) | Top 10 ranked shortlist |
| Wed | Design briefs | 3 briefs with prompts + keywords |
| Thu | Production | 3 designs generated + variants |
| Fri | Listing draft | 3 listings ready to publish |

### Signal Sources (30–45 min)
| Source | What to look for |
|---|---|
| Google Trends | Rising queries, seasonal spikes |
| Etsy/Redbubble | Bestseller tags, repeating motifs |
| Amazon Merch (search) | Category popularity + phrases |
| TikTok/Instagram | Visual motifs trending in last 7 days |
| Niche Reddit/Discord | Meme phrases, inside jokes |

### Validation Score (simple)
| Factor | Scale | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Demand | 1–5 | Search volume + trend slope |
| Competition | 1–5 | # listings; 1=low, 5=high |
| Longevity | 1–5 | Seasonal vs evergreen |
| Score | Demand + Longevity − Competition | Higher is better |

### Weekly Output Template
- **Top 10 Trend Shortlist** (title + niche + score)
- **Top 3 Design Briefs** (visual + text + style + keywords)
- **3 Listing Drafts** (title + tags + description)

## 3) Listing Standards (T-0024)

### Title Formula
**[Primary Keyword] + [Secondary Keyword] + [Use Case/Recipient] + [Style/Season]**

**Example:**
- "Retro Mountain Adventure Shirt – Hiking Graphic Tee for Outdoors Lovers"

### Tag Rules
- 12–15 tags total
- Mix: 5 broad, 5 niche, 2–3 seasonal/event, 1–2 long‑tail phrases

**Tag Template:**
- Broad: `graphic tee`, `tshirt`, `unisex tee`, `gift shirt`, `casual wear`
- Niche: `mountain shirt`, `hiking gift`, `outdoor lover`, `nature badge`
- Seasonal: `summer hiking`, `camping season`
- Long‑tail: `retro mountain adventure shirt`

### Description Template
**Hook (1–2 lines):**
> A bold [style] tee for [audience] who love [theme].

**Details (bullets):**
- Soft, lightweight fabric
- Unisex fit (true to size)
- High‑quality print, long‑lasting

**Occasions:**
> Great for [holiday], [event], or everyday wear.

**CTA:**
> Add to cart and wear your passion.
