# Color & Shape Hunt (Printable Lead Magnet)

**Goal:** Help kids (ages 3–6) identify basic colors + shapes through a simple scavenger hunt.

---

## Title
**Color & Shape Hunt!**

## Quick Instructions (Top of Page)
1) Look around the room or outside.
2) Find each color + shape.
3) Draw it or check the box.

---

## Colors to Find
- ⬜ Red
- ⬜ Blue
- ⬜ Yellow
- ⬜ Green
- ⬜ Orange
- ⬜ Purple

---

## Shapes to Find
- ⬜ Circle
- ⬜ Square
- ⬜ Triangle
- ⬜ Rectangle
- ⬜ Star
- ⬜ Heart

---

## Scavenger Grid (Draw or Check)
| Color | Find It | Shape | Find It |
|---|---|---|---|
| Red | ⬜ | Circle | ⬜ |
| Blue | ⬜ | Square | ⬜ |
| Yellow | ⬜ | Triangle | ⬜ |
| Green | ⬜ | Rectangle | ⬜ |
| Orange | ⬜ | Star | ⬜ |
| Purple | ⬜ | Heart | ⬜ |

---

## Bonus Round
Find **something** that is:
- ⬜ **Two colors** at once
- ⬜ **Two shapes** at once (example: a window = square + rectangle)

---

## Parent/Teacher Tip
Ask: “What color is it?” and “What shape is it?” Then have the child describe where they found it.
