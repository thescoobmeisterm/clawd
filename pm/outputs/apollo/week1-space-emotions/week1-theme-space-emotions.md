# Apollo’s Learning Corner — Week 1 Theme

**Theme:** Space & Emotions ("Apollo’s Feelings in Space")

## One‑sentence concept
Apollo explores space and learns to name his feelings while using simple tools to feel safe and brave.

## Learning Goals (Week 1)
1. Identify 4 core emotions: **happy, sad, scared, excited**.
2. Practice coping tools: **deep breaths**, **count to 5**, **ask for help**.
3. Connect emotions to body signals: **fast heart**, **tight tummy**, **big smile**.

## Vocabulary
Emotion words: happy, sad, scared, excited
Body signals: heart fast, tummy tight, smile
Tools: breathe, count, ask for help

## Story Beats (book anchor)
1. **Launch:** Apollo is excited to blast off.
2. **Drift:** He feels scared when his ship wobbles.
3. **Tool:** He breathes and counts to 5.
4. **Help:** He asks Mission Control for help.
5. **Win:** He feels proud and happy to complete the mission.

## Daily Outputs (5‑day plan)
- **Day 1:** Theme + goals + vocab (this doc)
- **Day 2:** Book beat sheet (10–12 pages)
- **Day 3:** Printable activity (feelings matching)
- **Day 4:** 60‑sec YouTube Short storyboard
- **Day 5:** Simple recap + checklist for next week

## Tone & Style Notes
- Warm, reassuring, curious
- Simple sentences, 1–2 ideas per page
- Repeat key phrases for learning retention

## CTA / Parent Prompt
“Ask your child: *What does your body feel like when you’re excited?*”
