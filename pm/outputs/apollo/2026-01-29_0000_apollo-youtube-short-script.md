# YouTube Short — Script + Storyboard (Apollo’s Learning Corner)

**Title:** “3…2…1… ROCKET!” (30–40 sec)

**Objective:** Build excitement for learning while reinforcing counting + space vocab.

---

## Script (VO + On‑Screen Text)

**0–3s**
- **Visual:** Quick montage of space (stars, moon, rocket).
- **VO:** “Ready for a space adventure?”
- **Text:** “SPACE ADVENTURE!”

**3–7s**
- **Visual:** Apollo the astronaut waves.
- **VO:** “I’m Apollo. Let’s count down!”
- **Text:** “COUNT DOWN!”

**7–12s**
- **Visual:** Big numbers 3…2…1 on screen.
- **VO:** “3…2…1…”
- **Text:** “3 2 1” (animated)

**12–16s**
- **Visual:** Rocket launches with whoosh.
- **VO:** “Blast off!”
- **Text:** “BLAST OFF!”

**16–24s**
- **Visual:** Stars appear; Apollo points to 5 stars.
- **VO:** “Can you count the stars? 1, 2, 3, 4, 5!”
- **Text:** “COUNT THE STARS”

**24–32s**
- **Visual:** Quick drawing of a rocket by a kid (hand + crayon).
- **VO:** “Draw your rocket and share it with us!”
- **Text:** “DRAW YOUR ROCKET”

**32–38s**
- **Visual:** Logo + URL.
- **VO:** “Learn and play with Apollo!”
- **Text:** “apollo’s learning corner”
