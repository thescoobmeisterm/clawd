# Math Adventures: Counting with Feelings

## Printable 1: "Counting My Feelings" Activity Sheet

### Description
A combined math + SEL printable where children count and identify different emotions. Features numbers 1-10 with corresponding emotion faces and an activity to draw their favorite feeling.

### Target Age
4-8 years

### Learning Objectives
- Counting practice (1-10)
- Emotion recognition vocabulary
- Self-awareness development

### Content
- Cover page with character (to be designed)
- Page 1: "I can count my feelings" - 10 emotion faces with numbers
- Page 2: "Draw your feeling" - blank face template
- Page 3: "How I feel today" - simple check-in chart
- Page 4: Bonus - color by emotion

### Pricing Strategy
- Single unit: $3.99
- Bundle of 5: $14.99 (recommended)
- Full pack (10 printables): $24.99
