# Pat the Firefighting Cat — Story Draft (12‑Page Picture Book)

## Cover
**Title:** Pat the Firefighting Cat
**Tagline:** Brave, kind, and always ready to help.

## Page‑by‑Page Script
| Page | Text | Illustration Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Pat lived at the firehouse, right beside the big red truck. | Cozy bunk + truck in background |
| 2 | Every morning, Pat checked his gear—boots, hat, and coat. | Pat in oversized gear |
| 3 | One day, the alarm rang! “Meow! Let’s go!” | Siren lights, excited crew |
| 4 | The fire was small, but the smoke was thick. Pat stayed calm. | Gentle smoke, not scary |
| 5 | Pat heard a tiny cry inside the house. | Little kitten hidden under table |
| 6 | “I’m coming!” Pat said, low and steady. | Pat crouching, focused |
| 7 | Pat carried the kitten out, safe and sound. | Firefighters cheering |
| 8 | The family smiled with happy tears. “Thank you, Pat!” | Warm, grateful scene |
| 9 | Back at the station, Pat cleaned his gear. | Quiet moment, pride |
|10 | That night, Pat wrote the kitten’s name in the rescue book. | Big logbook + paw print |
|11 | “Every rescue matters,” Pat purred. | Pat looking at moon |
|12 | And every day, Pat was ready to help again. | End with firehouse glow |

## Notes
- Keep tone gentle, reassuring, and brave.
- Add small recurring visual: a tiny paw‑print sticker that moves each page.
