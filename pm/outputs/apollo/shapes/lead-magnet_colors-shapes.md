# Color & Shape Hunt (Ages 3–7)

**Title:** Color & Shape Hunt!  
**Goal:** Parent‑guided activity to build color + shape recognition.  
**Duration:** 10–15 min  
**Setup:** Print 1–2 pages, grab crayons

---

## Instructions (one line)
Look around the house. Find the item, circle it, then color the box.

## Parent Tips (quick)
- Let kids choose *any* item that matches.
- Say the words out loud: “red circle,” “blue square.”
- Praise effort over perfection.

- **Extension:** Draw one item you found.
