# Apollo's Numbers Count-Along!
## YouTube Short Script (60 seconds)

---

**[OPEN: Space background, Apollo floating with jetpack]**

**APOLLO:** (cheerful, energetic)
"Hey friends! It's Apollo! Today we're going to count together - from ONE to TEN!"

---

**[COUNT 1]**
**APOLLO:** "Ready? ONE!"

*[Show: 1 rocket ship zooming by]*

**ALL:** "ONE!"

---

**[COUNT 2]**
**APOLLO:** "TWO!"

*[Show: 2 friendly aliens waving hello]*

**ALL:** "TWO!"

---

**[COUNT 3]**
**APOLLO:** "THREE!"

*[Show: 3 twinkling stars appearing]*

**ALL:** "THREE!"

---

**[COUNT 4]**
**APOLLO:** "FOUR!"

*[Show: 4 robot legs walking across screen]*

**ALL:** "FOUR!"

---

**[COUNT 5]**
**APOLLO:** "FIVE!"

*[Show: 5 high-fiving hands]*

**ALL:** "FIVE!"

---

**[COUNT 6]**
**APOLLO:** "SIX!"

*[Show: 6 eggs in a nest, one wiggling]*

**ALL:** "SIX!"

---

**[COUNT 7]**
**APOLLO:** "SEVEN!"

*[Show: 7 colorful planets spinning]*

**ALL:** "SEVEN!"

---

**[COUNT 8]**
**APOLLO:** "EIGHT!"

*[Show: 8 wiggly octopus tentacles]*

**ALL:** "EIGHT!"

---

**[COUNT 9]**
**APOLLO:** "NINE!"

*[Show: 9 balloons floating up]*

**ALL:** "NINE!"

---

**[COUNT 10]**
**APOLLO:** "Andddd... TEN!"

*[Show: 10 toes wiggling, then Apollo's face pops in]*

**ALL:** "TEN!"

---

**[CLOSE]**
**APOLLO:** (big smile)
"Great counting, space explorers! Which number was YOUR favorite? Comment below!"

*[Show: SUBSCRIBE button animation]*

**APOLLO:** "Subscribe for more space adventures with Apollo!"

**[END CARD: "Subscribe for more!" with subscribe button]**

---

## Audio Notes
- Background music: Upbeat, space-themed instrumental
- Sound effects: Whoosh for transitions, ding for correct counts
- Voice: Child-friendly, enthusiastic
- Pacing: SLOW down for young viewers to repeat

## Visual Notes
- Bright, saturated colors
- Smooth animations (no jerky movements)
- Characters should have expressive faces
- Numbers should be large and clearly visible

## Estimated Runtime
- Without pauses: ~25 seconds
- With pauses for kids to repeat: ~50-60 seconds
