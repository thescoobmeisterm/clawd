## Design Guidelines

### Colors (Palette)
| Emotion | Color | Hex |
|---------|-------|-----|
| Happy | Warm Yellow | #FFD93D |
| Sad | Soft Blue | #6BCB77 |
| Angry | Soft Red | #FF6B6B |
| Calm | Mint Green | #4D96FF |
| Excited | Bright Orange | #FF9F43 |
| Surprised | Light Purple | #A29BFE |
| Lonely | Gray Blue | #74B9FF |
| Worried | Soft Teal | #81ECEC |

### Typography
- **Headers**: "Comic Sans MS" or "Fredoka One" (playful, readable)
- **Body text**: "Open Sans" or "Comic Sans MS"
- **Numbers**: Large, traceable (30pt+)
- **Lines**: 1.5pt stroke for tracing

### Character Design
- Friendly owl named "County" or friendly bear named "Buddy"
- Simple shapes (circle head, oval body)
- Expressive eyes and mouths
- Consistent style across all pages

## Print Specifications
- **Size**: US Letter (8.5" x 11")
- **Format**: PDF for printing
- **Resolution**: 300 DPI
- **Color**: CMYK for print
- **Margins**: 0.5" minimum
