# Apollo’s Learning Corner — Week 2 Beat Sheet

**Theme:** Community & Kindness ("Apollo Helps His Crew")
**Length:** 10–12 pages

## Beat Sheet (12 pages)
1. **Cover:** Apollo and friends in the ship, waving.
2. **Setup:** Apollo is excited to help the crew today.
3. **Problem Appears:** A tool goes missing during a repair.
4. **Feelings:** The crew feels worried; Apollo feels a little nervous.
5. **Offer Help:** Apollo says, “Can I help?”
6. **Team Plan:** They decide to search together.
7. **Encouragement:** Apollo cheers: “We can do it!”
8. **Discovery:** The tool is found tucked under a crate.
9. **Fix It:** The crew uses the tool to finish the repair.
10. **Thanks:** The crew says, “Thanks, Apollo.”
11. **Lesson:** Apollo feels proud; kindness helps everyone.
12. **Wrap/CTA:** “How can you help a friend today?”

## Repeating Phrases (for learning)
- “Can I help?”
- “Let’s do it together.”
- “Thank you.”

## Visual Notes
- Show teamwork moments (sharing, pointing, helping lift)
- Warm expressions: smiling, relieved, proud
