# Apollo’s Learning Corner — Week 2 Theme

**Theme:** Community & Kindness ("Apollo Helps His Crew")

## One‑sentence concept
Apollo works with his crew to solve small problems, learning how kindness and teamwork help everyone succeed.

## Learning Goals (Week 2)
1. Identify kind actions: **help, share, encourage, thank**.
2. Practice teamwork language: **“Can I help?” “Let’s do it together.”**
3. Build empathy: notice how others feel and respond kindly.

## Vocabulary
Kind actions: help, share, encourage, thank
Teamwork: together, team, friend, crew
Feelings: proud, grateful, calm

## Story Beats (book anchor)
1. **Problem:** A tool goes missing on the ship.
2. **Try:** Apollo offers help to search.
3. **Team:** Crew splits tasks and encourages each other.
4. **Solve:** They find the tool and fix the issue.
5. **Celebrate:** Apollo thanks the crew and feels proud.

## Daily Outputs (5‑day plan)
- **Day 1:** Theme + goals + vocab (this doc)
- **Day 2:** Book beat sheet (10–12 pages)
- **Day 3:** Printable activity (kindness bingo)
- **Day 4:** 60‑sec YouTube Short storyboard
- **Day 5:** Simple recap + checklist for next week

## Tone & Style Notes
- Friendly, cooperative, encouraging
- Simple sentences, positive framing

## CTA / Parent Prompt
“Ask your child: *How can we help someone today?*”
