**Format:** 8.5x11 portrait (printable PDF)

**Apollo’s Space Explorer Passport**

**Trace the words:**

**Spell it:**
- My name: _____________
- My spaceship name: _____________

### Section B — Count the Stars (right column)
Draw **10 stars** inside the window.

[Big blank circle/oval window for drawing]

- [ ] I can count to 10
- [ ] I can name 3 planets

- [ ] I can say “blast off!”

### Section D — Color Zone (full width)
**Color Apollo’s Rocket!**
[Large rocket outline]

### Footer (small text)

## LAYOUT NOTES (for designer)

- Keep 0.25" internal margins; 0.5" outer margins
- Make the rocket outline large enough to color (centered)
- Use soft accent colors (space blue, purple, yellow)
