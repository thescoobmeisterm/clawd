# Apollo’s Learning Corner — YouTube Short (Script + Storyboard)

**Length:** 30–40 seconds
**Theme:** “Find the Hidden Star” (quick visual search + counting)
**Style:** Bright, playful, simple SFX

---

## Script (VO)
**0–3s**
“Hey Explorers! Can you help Apollo find the hidden star?”

**4–10s**
“Look closely… the star is **hiding** on Planet Purple!”

**11–20s**
“Point to the star! Now count with me: 1…2…3…4…5!”

**21–30s**
“Great job! You’re a Junior Space Explorer. See you next mission!”

---

## Storyboard (shot list)
| Time | Visual | On‑screen Text | SFX/Music |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0–3s | Apollo waves beside 3 planets (red, green, purple). | “Find the Hidden Star!” | Soft whoosh + sparkle cue |
| 4–10s | Camera zooms toward purple planet; star peeks out. | “Is it here?” | “Peek!” sparkle |
| 11–20s | Star fully visible; count numbers pop up (1–5). | “Count the stars!” | Count beeps (1–5) |
| 21–30s | Apollo thumbs‑up; rocket launches. | “Junior Explorer!” | Rocket whoosh |

---

“Find the Star!”

## Notes
- Keep visuals simple and bold for small screens.
- Use 2–3 core colors max.
