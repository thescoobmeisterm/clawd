## Video 1: "Apollo's Shape Hunt"

**Duration:** 3-4 minutes  
**Learning Objective:** Identify basic shapes (circle, square, triangle, rectangle)  
**SEL Focus:** Curiosity and exploration

### Scene Breakdown

| Scene | Visual | Audio | Duration |
|-------|--------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Apollo in spaceship looking at Earth | "Apollo here! Today we're going on a shape hunt!" | 15s |
| 2 | Earth appears with shape outlines highlighting | "From space, Earth looks like a big blue circle!" | 20s |
| 3 | Apollo lands in a backyard | "But shapes are EVERYWHERE! Let's look closer." | 15s |
| 4 | Close-up of a wagon wheel | "What's this? A circle! Can you say circle?" | 20s |
| 5 | Pointing at a house | "A house has a triangle on top! Triangle!" | 20s |
| 6 | Showing a window | "A window is a rectangle. Rectangle!" | 20s |
| 7 | Finding a block | "And this square block! Say it with me: SQUARE!" | 25s |
| 8 | Apollo dancing with shapes | "Shapes, shapes, everywhere! Circle, square, triangle, rectangle!" | 30s |
| 9 | Wrap-up | "Great shape hunting today! What shapes did YOU find?" | 20s |

### Full Script

**[0:00] INTRO**
*Apollo floats in space with Earth behind him*
**Apollo:** "Hi friends! It's Apollo, your alien friend from far, far away! Today, I'm going to show you something amazing. From way up here, Earth looks like... what do you think it looks like?"

**[0:15]**
*Earth zooms in*
**Apollo:** "A BIG BLUE CIRCLE! A circle is round all the way around, like a ball! Can you make a circle with your arms? Try it!"

**[0:30]**
*Apollo lands in a backyard*
**Apollo:** "But circles aren't just in space! They're EVERYWHERE! Let's go on a SHAPE HUNT! Follow me!"

**[0:45]**
*Camera pans to a wagon*
**Apollo:** "Oh! Look at this! A wagon wheel! It's a PERFECT circle! Can you find something circle-shaped at your house?"

**[1:00]**
*House appears*
**Apollo:** "Now look at this house! What's on TOP of the house? A TRIANGLE! It goes to a point! Can you make a triangle with your fingers? Try it!"

**[1:15]**
*Window close-up*
**Apollo:** "And look HERE! A window! It's a RECTANGLE! Longer one way than the other. Like a door, or a phone screen!"

**[1:30]**
*Apollo picks up a wooden block*
**Apollo:** "And here's one of my FAVORITES! A SQUARE! All four sides are the same length. Can you find a square?"

**[1:45]**
*Montage of shapes around the yard*
**Apollo:** "Shapes, shapes, EVERYWHERE! Circle, square, triangle, rectangle! Say it with me!"

**[2:00]**
*Apollo dancing*
**Apollo:** "Circle, square, triangle, rectangle! [dances] Circle, square, triangle, rectangle!"

**[2:20]**
*Apollo back at spaceship*
**Apollo:** "You did AMAZING on our shape hunt! Now I want to know... what shapes did YOU find today? Tell a grown-up and point to them!"

**[2:40]**
*Apollo waving*
**Apollo:** "Remember, the world is full of shapes if you look closely! Bye friends! Catch you on the flip side!"

**[END CARD]**
"Subscribe for more Apollo adventures! What shapes will we find next?"

## Video 2: "Apollo's Feelings Face"

**Duration:** 3-4 minutes  
**Learning Objective:** Identify and name basic emotions (happy, sad, angry, scared)  
**SEL Focus:** Emotional awareness and expression

### Scene Breakdown

| Scene | Visual | Audio | Duration |
|-------|--------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Apollo looking at camera | "Sometimes I feel HAPPY! Sometimes I feel SAD!" | 15s |
| 2 | Show 4 faces in grid | "Let's learn about feelings together!" | 15s |
| 3 | Happy Apollo with big smile | "When I feel HAPPY, I smile big! My eyes crinkle!" | 25s |
| 4 | Sad Apollo with frown | "When I feel SAD, I might cry. That's okay!" | 25s |
| 5 | Angry Apollo with furrowed brows | "When I'm ANGRY, I feel hot! My brow goes down!" | 25s |
| 6 | Scared Apollo with wide eyes | "When I'm SCARED, my eyes get big! My heart goes fast!" | 25s |
| 7 | Mirror interaction | "What does YOUR face look like when you feel [emotion]?" | 30s |
| 8 | Wrap-up | "All feelings are okay. It's okay to feel anything!" | 20s |

### Full Script

**[0:00] INTRO**
*Apollo looks at camera with neutral expression*
**Apollo:** "Hi friends! Do you know something cool? I have feelings! And SO DO YOU! Today we're going to learn about our FEELINGS FACE!"

**[0:15]**
*Grid of 4 emotion faces appears*
**Apollo:** "Everyone has a feelings face. It shows others how we feel inside! Let's learn what each feeling looks like!"

**[0:30]**
*Happy face with big smile, crinkly eyes*
**Apollo:** "This is HAPPY! See my smile? It's so big my eyes CRINKLE up! How do YOU look when you're happy? Do you smile? Do you giggle?"

**[0:55]**
*Sad face with frown, tear*
**Apollo:** "This is SAD. Sometimes I feel sad. Maybe I lost a toy or missed my friend. When I'm sad, my mouth goes down and I might cry. And that's okay! Crying is how our feelings come out."

**[1:20]**
*Angry face with furrowed brows*
**Apollo:** "This is ANGRY. When I'm angry, I feel hot inside! My eyebrows go DOWN and my mouth gets squiggly. It's okay to be angry, but we use words to tell people how we feel."

**[1:45]**
*Scared face with wide eyes*
**Apollo:** "This is SCARED. When I'm scared, my eyes get REALLY big! My heart goes thump-thump-thump fast! Maybe I heard a loud noise or saw something surprising. It's okay to be scared. You can ask for a hug!"

**[2:10]**
*Apollo with mirror*
**Apollo:** "Now let's check YOUR feelings face! Make a HAPPY face! [pause] Make a SAD face! [pause] Make an ANGRY face! [pause] Make a SCARED face! [pause] You did GREAT!"

**[2:40]**
*Apollo sincere*
**Apollo:** "Here's something important: ALL feelings are okay. There's no bad feelings. You can feel happy, sad, angry, or scared. And it's always okay to talk to a grown-up about how you feel."

**[3:00]**
*Apollo waving*
**Apollo:** "You're a feelings expert now! Which feeling do you feel most today? Tell a grown-up! Bye friends!"

## Video 3: "Apollo Counts to Ten"

**Duration:** 3-4 minutes  
**Learning Objective:** Count to 10 with one-to-one correspondence  
**SEL Focus:** Patience and persistence

### Scene Breakdown

| Scene | Visual | Audio | Duration |
|-------|--------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Apollo with counting blocks | "I'm going to count to ten! Will you count with me?" | 15s |
| 2-11 | Show 1-10 with visual representation | Counting each number with blocks/stars/etc | 15s each |
| 12 | All 10 items together | "We counted to TEN! That's a big number!" | 20s |
| 13 | Fast replay | "Let's count AGAIN, faster this time!" | 20s |
| 14 | Wrap-up | "You did it! You're a counting champion!" | 15s |

### Full Script

**[0:00] INTRO**
*Apollo sitting with pile of blocks and stars*
**Apollo:** "Hi friends! I'm so excited! You know what? I'm going to count to TEN today! That's a REALLY big number! Will you count with me?"

**[0:15]**
*Shows 1 block*
**Apollo:** "One! Can you say ONE? [pause] One!"

**[0:30]**
*Shows 2 stars*
**Apollo:** "Two! Two stars! TWO!"

**[0:45]**
*Shows 3 hearts*
**Apollo:** "Three! Three hearts! THREE!"

**[1:00]**
*Shows 4 circles*
**Apollo:** "Four! Four circles! FOUR!"

**[1:15]**
*Shows 5 triangles*
**Apollo:** "Five! Five triangles! FIVE!"

**[1:30]**
*Shows 6 squares*
**Apollo:** "Six! Six squares! SIX!"

**[1:45]**
*Shows 7 moons*
**Apollo:** "Seven! Seven moons! SEVEN!"

**[2:00]**
*Shows 8 diamonds*
**Apollo:** "Eight! Eight diamonds! EIGHT!"

**[2:15]**
*Shows 9 ovals*
**Apollo:** "Nine! Nine ovals! NINE!"

**[2:30]**
*Shows 10 lightning bolts*
**Apollo:** "And TEN! Ten lightning bolts! TEN!"

**[2:45]**
*All 10 items shown together*
**Apollo:** "WE DID IT! We counted all the way to TEN! That's a big achievement! Pat yourself on the back!"

**[3:00]**
*Fast replay montage*
**Apollo:** "Let's count again, but FASTER this time! ONE, TWO, THREE, FOUR, FIVE, SIX, SEVEN, EIGHT, NINE, TEN! [claps] YOU'RE A COUNTING CHAMPION!"

**[3:20]**
*Apollo excited*
**Apollo:** "Now you can count to ten too! Count something at your house. How many toys do you have? How many toes? Count them all! Bye friends!"
